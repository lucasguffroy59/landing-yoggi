FAQ

Questions about AI chatbots for kids

What is an AI chatbot for kids?
An AI chatbot for kids is a conversational AI designed specifically for children rather than adults — with age-adapted vocabulary, voice support for non-readers, refusal of inappropriate content by design, and visibility for parents into what their child is discussing. Yoggi is one example, built for ages 3 to 15.
Can a young child chat with an AI chatbot by voice?
Yes, with a chatbot designed for it. Yoggi supports full voice input and output, so children as young as 3 — who can't yet read or type — can have a complete conversation by speaking and listening.
Does an AI chatbot for kids tell parents what was discussed?
The best ones do. Yoggi gives parents a full, PIN-protected chat history, a daily AI-generated insight, an optional weekly email recap, and an immediate email alert if a conversation ever needs attention.
