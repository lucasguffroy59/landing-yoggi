How It Works

The chat's own safety rules, a fixed server-side style template (cute cartoon look, nothing scary, no text, no realistic people, no copyrighted characters), and the image provider's own filters — all three, every time.

Every image comes out in a consistent, cute cartoon look suited to the child's age — never realistic, never frightening, by design rather than by luck.

FAQ

Common questions about Yoggi's AI drawings

Can Yoggi generate images for kids?
Yes. When a child asks to see something or asks Yoggi to draw it, Yoggi can generate a cartoon-style picture right inside the chat, in a rounded frame under its reply.
Is AI image generation safe for children?
Yes. Every image request goes through three layers of safety: the conversation's own child-safe rules, a fixed server-side style template enforcing a cute, age-appropriate cartoon look with no scary content, no text, no realistic people, and no copyrighted characters, and the image provider's own built-in safety filters.
How many images can my child generate?
Free accounts get 5 images for life. Premium accounts get 15 images per day. Only images that are actually generated count toward the quota — a failed attempt doesn't use it up.
What happens if the image generation fails?
Yoggi shows a gentle message like "my drawing didn't work, let's try again later" instead of the image, and never blames the child or explains the technical reason.
