What is the best AI for kids?
The best AI for kids is one built specifically for children, not an adult tool with a filter applied afterward. Look for age-adapted responses, voice input for non-readers, real-time safety alerts to parents, and a parent dashboard — not just content filtering. Yoggi is built around these four principles for ages 3 to 15.
Is AI safe for kids to use?
It depends entirely on which AI. General-purpose tools like ChatGPT or Gemini were designed for adults and have no age-awareness. An AI purpose-built for children, like Yoggi, refuses inappropriate content by design, adapts its language to the child's age, and gives parents real-time alerts and full visibility into conversations.
What age can a child start using AI?
With the right tool, from age 3. Voice input lets children who can't yet read or type have a conversation by speaking, with responses using the simplest possible vocabulary. Yoggi supports children from age 3 through 15, adjusting automatically as they grow.
