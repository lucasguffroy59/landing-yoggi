FAQ

Common questions about AI voice calls for kids

Can my child have a real voice conversation with an AI?
Yes. Yoggi has a real-time voice call mode: your child taps a phone button and talks to Yoggi live, with continuous two-way audio, no text on screen at all. It's built for children who think and express themselves better out loud than by typing.
Is a live AI voice call safe for children?
Yes. The same age-adapted, safety-first behavior that governs Yoggi's text chat applies to voice calls. After each call, the conversation is analyzed for safety just like a text conversation, and parents are alerted immediately if anything needs their attention.
Can parents review what was said during a voice call?
Yes. The full transcript of every voice call is available to the parent in the read-only history, alongside the call's duration. The child sees that a call happened in their own conversation feed, but cannot reopen or replay its content — only the parent can.
Is the voice call feature free?
Free accounts get 5 cumulative minutes of voice calling for life. Premium accounts get 10 minutes per day. Once the quota is reached, the app offers to upgrade instead of starting the call.
