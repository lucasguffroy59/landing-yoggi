FAQ

Common questions about Yoggi's Free Play / Learning Mode

Can I make my child's AI conversations more educational?
Yes. Yoggi has a parent-only setting with two behaviors: Free Play, where Yoggi chats about anything the child brings up and can teach things along the way without ever forcing it, and Learning Mode, where Yoggi always steers conversations back toward reflection and discovery instead of purely recreational chat.
Who controls Free Play vs Learning Mode?
Only the parent. It's chosen once during setup and can be changed at any time from settings — the child never sees or controls this setting.
Does Learning Mode change Yoggi's safety rules?
No. Age-adapted responses and Yoggi's baseline safety rules apply identically in both Free Play and Learning Mode. Only the conversational focus changes, never the safety behavior.
Does this apply to voice calls too?
Yes. The Free Play / Learning Mode setting applies to both text chat and Yoggi's real-time voice calls, consistently.
